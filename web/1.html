Task 1


\(m/s\) \(\circ\) \(m\) \(m/s^2\)

Explanation

  1. Given a launch speed \(u\) and launch angle \(\theta\), calculate the \(x\) and \(y\) velocities.

    \( u_{x}=u \times cos(\theta) \)

    \( u_{y}=u \times sin(\theta) \)

  2. Calculate \(x\) and \(y\) coordinates for each point along the graph, increasing by \(\Delta t\) (preset to 0.1).

    1. \( x = u_{x} \times t \)

    2. \( y = h + u_{y} \times t - {{g \times t^2} \over 2} \)

  3. Plot the \(x\) and \(y\) coordinates onto the graph, extending the curve until \(y \le 0\).
